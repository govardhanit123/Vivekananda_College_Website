Course Of Study

Candidate shall choose any two-school subjects offered by the College for specialization in teaching under Ed6 and Ed7.

One of the subjects chosen for specialization must have been studied as an optional.

If the other subject chosen for specilization has not been studied as an optional, the candidate is required to undergo a specially designed content course in that subject outside the regular working hours by paying a special fee.

The candidates shall be examined only internally, as stipulated in the prescribed content course.

Note: Content course will be offered only in Kannada, English, History and Geography.

Candidates shall not be permitted to opt for more than one language for specilization (under Ed.4,5) unless they have studied one language as optional.
